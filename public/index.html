Ultimate CAD

INFORMATION ABOUT THE CAD

Ultimate CAD is a comprehensive Computer Aided Dispatch system designed for roleplay communities.


Features include:


🚔 LEO CAD

Full law enforcement dispatch with calls, BOLOs, reports, warrants, and search.

🚒 F&R CAD

Fire & Rescue dispatch with medical and incident reports.

🚧 DOT CAD

Department of Transportation dispatch with tow reports.

📡 Dispatcher CAD

Multi-agency dispatcher view with unit management.
